Homemade Chinese Chili Crisp

A savory, spicy, crunchy chili crisp built by slowly simmering garlic and whole spices in oil, then pouring the hot infused oil over peanuts, ginger, dried onions, chili flakes, and sesame seeds.

Before You Cook

Use caution

/!\ WARNING: This is a garlic-in-oil condiment. The creator's own gift guide says the finished chili crisp should be stored in the fridge, not at room temperature. Do not treat this entry as a shelf-stable preserve.

Instructions

  1. Slowly simmer the garlic in oil with whole spices so the garlic cooks without burning and the oil becomes aromatic.
  2. Prepare a heatproof bowl with peanuts, ginger, dried onions, chili flakes, and sesame seeds.
  3. When the oil is infused and the garlic is fried, pour the hot oil over the bowl ingredients so they sizzle.
  4. Stir in the umami-rich additions and the fried garlic.
  5. Cool, transfer to a clean jar, and refrigerate.

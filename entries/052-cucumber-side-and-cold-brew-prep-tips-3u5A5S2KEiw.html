Cucumber Side and Cold Brew Prep Tips

Two short kitchen prep ideas: a marinated cucumber side dish with soy, sesame, rice vinegar, chili oil, and optional MSG/sesame seeds; and a cold brew coffee concentrate made at a 1:5 coffee-to-water ratio by weight.

Reference

Summary

Two short kitchen prep ideas: a marinated cucumber side dish with soy, sesame, rice vinegar, chili oil, and optional MSG/sesame seeds; and a cold brew coffee concentrate made at a 1:5 coffee-to-water ratio by weight.

Key Points

  • For the cucumber side, slice 3 cucumbers and season with rice vinegar, sesame oil, salt, soy sauce, sugar, chili oil, optional MSG, and optional sesame seeds.
  • The cucumber side is best after resting in the refrigerator.
  • The source says to keep it refrigerated for less than 5 days.
  • For cold brew, use about 56 g dark roast coffee with 300 ml filtered water.
  • Steep for 18 hours.
  • Strain through cheesecloth, then a coffee filter for a cleaner result.
  • Dilute the concentrate with water to taste.

Practical Use

  • Treat the cucumber side as a flexible condiment or meal-prep side rather than a measured recipe.
  • Make cold brew concentrate ahead when time is available; the active work is low even though the steep is long.

Sundried Tomato Feta Tortilla Pockets

A quick air-fryer pocket made by folding feta, sundried tomatoes, basil, and sundried tomato oil into a tortilla, brushing it with egg wash, topping with sesame seeds, and finishing with honey and basil.

Instructions

  1. Slice the feta.
  2. Place a tortilla on the work surface.
  3. Add feta, sundried tomatoes, basil, and a little sundried tomato oil.
  4. Fold into a pocket.
  5. Brush with egg wash.
  6. Top with sesame seeds.
  7. Air fry for 8-10 minutes, until crisp and hot.
  8. Finish with honey and more fresh basil.

Notes

  • The source says puff pastry would also work, but uses a tortilla for speed.
  • Comments point out that this is still more involved than some people's "too lazy to cook" meal; keep it filed as a quick assembled meal rather than a true no-cook option.

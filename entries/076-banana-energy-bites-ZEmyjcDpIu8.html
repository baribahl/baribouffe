Banana Energy Bites

No-bake banana, peanut butter, protein powder, oat, honey, and chocolate chip snack bites that set in the fridge.

Cookable Untested

Ingredients

Makes about 18 bites:

  • 1 ripe banana, about 120 g peeled
  • 130 g creamy peanut butter
  • 30 g protein powder
  • 42 g honey
  • 160 g crunchy dark chocolate oats or rolled oats
  • 85 g mini chocolate chips

Effort

45 min including chilling

2 / 5 difficulty

low cleanup · 2 / 5

Instructions

  1. Mash the banana in a large bowl with a fork.
  2. Add peanut butter, protein powder, and honey.
  3. Mix with the fork until combined.
  4. Fold in the oats and chocolate chips with a spoon or spatula until evenly incorporated.
  5. Use a small spring-loaded scoop or spoon to portion about 18 balls.
  6. Roll with your hands.
  7. Cover and refrigerate for 30 minutes to set. If the mixture is sticky, chill before rolling.
  8. Enjoy chilled.

Notes

  • The video tests mixing on a flat surface, but the uploader comment gives the bowl method as the clear recipe.
  • Refrigerate for up to 5 days.
  • If using unflavored rolled oats instead of the listed crunchy dark chocolate oat product, taste before rolling; sweetness and texture may change.
Source notes

Source Notes

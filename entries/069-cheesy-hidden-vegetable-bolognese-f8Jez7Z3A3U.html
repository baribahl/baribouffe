Cheesy Hidden Vegetable Bolognese

A high-vegetable meat sauce built like a bolognese: minced celery, carrot, zucchini, onion, and tomato are cooked down with beef or a meat substitute, broth, rice vinegar, herbs, parmesan, and spices.

Cookable Use caution

Ingredients

For about 6 servings:

  • 400 g celery
  • 500 g carrot
  • 350 g zucchini
  • 380 g onion
  • 700 g tomato
  • 400 g beef or meat substitute
  • 480 ml broth
  • 30 ml rice vinegar
  • Oregano, to taste
  • Parsley, to taste
  • Parmesan, to taste
  • Chili flakes, to taste
  • Garlic powder, to taste
  • Pasta of choice, for serving

Before You Cook

Use caution

/!\ WARNING: If freezing, cool the sauce quickly in shallow containers before it goes into the fridge or freezer. Reheat portions until fully hot throughout.

Instructions

  1. Mince the celery, carrot, zucchini, onion, and tomato in a food processor.
  2. Saute the minced vegetables until they start to soften and lose moisture.
  3. Brown the beef or meat substitute.
  4. Add the tomatoes if they were kept separate from the first vegetable batch.
  5. Add broth, rice vinegar, oregano, parsley, parmesan, chili flakes, and garlic powder.
  6. Simmer for about 2 hours, stirring as needed, until the vegetables collapse into a thick sauce.
  7. Serve with pasta, or portion the sauce for freezing.

Notes

Nutella Yogurt Bites

Frozen yogurt and chocolate-hazelnut scoops dipped in dark chocolate and finished with sea salt and crunchy nuts. Let them thaw slightly before eating for a creamier texture.

Cookable Untested

Ingredients

  • 250 g Greek yogurt
  • 85 g Nutella or another chocolate-hazelnut spread
  • About 6 g cocoa powder
  • 10 ml maple syrup or honey, optional
  • 170 g dark chocolate chips
  • About 7 ml coconut oil
  • Sea salt
  • Crunchy nuts, chopped, for topping

Effort

1.5-2.5 hr including freezing

2 / 5 difficulty

medium cleanup · 3 / 5

Source

Giulia Ardizzone

Instructions

  1. Mix the yogurt, Nutella, cocoa powder, and optional maple syrup or honey until well combined.
  2. Portion the mixture into scoops on a parchment-lined baking sheet.
  3. Freeze for 1-2 hours, until firm.
  4. Melt the dark chocolate with the coconut oil until smooth.
  5. Dip each frozen scoop into the melted chocolate, fully coating it.
  6. Lift with a fork and let excess chocolate drip off.
  7. Sprinkle with sea salt and chopped nuts while the chocolate is still wet.
  8. Return to the freezer or refrigerator for a few minutes to set.
  9. Let defrost slightly before serving for a creamier texture.

Notes

  • The source says any chocolate-hazelnut spread can replace Nutella.
  • These can be set briefly in the freezer or refrigerator after coating.

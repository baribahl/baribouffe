Fondant Potatoes

Thick potato cylinders are soaked, dried, seared, basted with butter and herbs, then braised halfway submerged in stock in a hot oven until fork-tender.

Needs details

Ingredients

  • Russet potatoes, quantity not specified
  • Salt
  • High-smoke-point oil, such as avocado oil
  • Butter, quantity not specified
  • Fresh thyme
  • Garlic cloves, crushed
  • Fresh rosemary
  • Chicken stock or vegetable stock, enough to half-submerge the potatoes

Instructions

  1. Cut russet potatoes into 2.5 cm thick rounds.
  2. Use a ring mold to cut the rounds into neat cylinders.
  3. Soak the potato rounds in water for 20 minutes to remove excess starch.
  4. Pat thoroughly dry with paper towel.
  5. Season with salt.
  6. Heat oil in a stainless steel pan.
  7. Sear the potatoes for 2-3 minutes on the first side, until golden brown.
  8. Flip the potatoes.
  9. Reduce the heat to low.
  10. Add butter, thyme, crushed garlic, and rosemary.
  11. When the butter is frothy, add stock until the potatoes are halfway submerged.
  12. Transfer to a 200 C oven for 20 minutes.
  13. Serve when fork-tender and soft inside.

Notes

  • Drying the potatoes thoroughly helps prevent sticking.
  • Vegetable stock keeps the dish vegetarian.
  • The potato trimmings can be saved for mash, soup, or hash.

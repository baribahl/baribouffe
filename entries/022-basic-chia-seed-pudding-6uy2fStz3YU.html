Basic Chia Seed Pudding

A simple high-protein chia pudding base with fruit, Greek yogurt, milk, and a small topping such as dark chocolate, cocoa nibs, or peanut butter. The source emphasizes keeping the base simple and varying the fruit or mix-ins.

Cookable Untested

Ingredients

  • 30 g chia seeds
  • 100 g mashed or chopped fruit
  • 150 g 0% Greek yogurt
  • 200 ml semi-skimmed milk
  • 10 g dark chocolate, cocoa nibs, or peanut butter

Optional:

  • Honey
  • Vanilla
  • Cinnamon

Effort

5 min plus chilling

1 / 5 difficulty

low cleanup · 1 / 5

Source

The Plant Slant

Instructions

  1. Combine the chia seeds, fruit, Greek yogurt, and milk.
  2. Add optional honey, vanilla, or cinnamon if desired.
  3. Mix well.
  4. Cover and refrigerate until set, ideally at least 2 hours or overnight.
  5. Top with dark chocolate, cocoa nibs, or peanut butter.

Notes

  • The creator notes that sweetener may be needed if using unsweetened yogurt.
  • The pinned comment gives the complete ingredient quantities.
  • The recipe is meant as a flexible breakfast base, not a fancy dessert.

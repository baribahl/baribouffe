Korean Spinach Banchan

A quick Korean spinach side dish: briefly blanch spinach, shock it in ice water, squeeze out excess water, then season with sesame oil, soy sauce, garlic, green onion, gochugaru, sesame seeds, and salt.

Needs details

Ingredients

Per person:

  • About 140 g fresh spinach [review: source says one box]
  • Salt, for the blanching water and seasoning
  • Sesame oil, to taste
  • Soy sauce, to taste
  • Minced garlic, to taste
  • Green onion, finely sliced, to taste
  • Gochugaru, to taste
  • Sesame seeds, to taste

Before You Cook

Needs details

Instructions

  1. Bring salted water to a boil.
  2. Add the spinach and blanch for 20 seconds, just until bright green and wilted.
  3. Transfer immediately to an ice bath.
  4. Drain the spinach.
  5. Gently squeeze out excess water by hand.
  6. Season with sesame oil, soy sauce, minced garlic, green onion, gochugaru, sesame seeds, and salt.
  7. Mix well and serve as banchan.

Notes

  • The ice bath is important because the spinach overcooks quickly.
  • The source frames this as a 5-minute side dish.

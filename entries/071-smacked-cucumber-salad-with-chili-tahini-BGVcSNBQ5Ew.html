Smacked Cucumber Salad with Chili Tahini

Before You Cook

Needs details

/!\ DISCLAIMER: The short gives the components and assembly order, but not the quantities. Treat this as a entry method note until the dressing and tahini base are tested in metric amounts.

Instructions

  1. Smack the cucumbers so they split and absorb dressing more easily.
  2. Roughly chop the cucumbers.
  3. Salt them and let them sit briefly.
  4. Mix soy sauce, rice wine vinegar, garlic, lime juice, and sesame seeds into a dressing.
  5. Dress the cucumbers.
  6. Mix tahini with crispy chili oil and a splash of lime juice for the base.
  7. Spread the chili tahini on the plate.
  8. Add the dressed cucumbers and finish with the crispy garnish.

Notes

  • Smacking the cucumbers is functional: the cracked surfaces hold dressing better than clean slices.
  • If the garnish is actually crispy gyoza, it may not be vegan; use fried wonton strips or roasted peanuts as a simpler alternative.

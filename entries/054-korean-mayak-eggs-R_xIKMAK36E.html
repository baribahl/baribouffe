Korean Mayak Eggs

Soft-boiled eggs marinated overnight in a soy-based liquid with green onion, ginger, red chili, garlic, onion, rice vinegar, honey, sesame oil, sesame seeds, and water. Serve over rice with extra marinade.

Instructions

  1. Boil the eggs for 7 minutes.
  2. Transfer the eggs to ice water.
  3. Chop the green onion, ginger, red chili, garlic, and onion.
  4. Put the aromatics in a container.
  5. Add soy sauce, rice vinegar, honey, sesame oil, sesame seeds, and water.
  6. Peel the eggs.
  7. Place the eggs in the marinade, making sure they are submerged.
  8. Cover and refrigerate overnight.
  9. Serve with rice and some of the marinade.

Notes

  • The creator likes rolling the boiled eggs gently to crack and peel them.
  • The 7-minute boil is meant to give a jammy yolk.

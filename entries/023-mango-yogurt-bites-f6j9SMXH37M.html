Mango Yogurt Bites

A frozen snack made by mixing chopped mango with yogurt and honey, freezing the mixture as dollops, then dipping the frozen pieces in melted white chocolate loosened with coconut oil. The tested toppings are shredded coconut and matcha.

Instructions

  1. Peel and chop the mangoes.
  2. Mix yogurt with a drizzle of honey.
  3. Fold the chopped mango into the yogurt mixture.
  4. Spoon large dollops onto a parchment-lined tray.
  5. Freeze for a couple of hours, until solid enough to dip.
  6. Melt white chocolate with a little coconut oil over hot water.
  7. Dip the frozen mango-yogurt pieces in the melted white chocolate.
  8. Add shredded coconut immediately, before the shell sets, or dust with matcha.
  9. Return to the freezer if needed until firm.

Notes

  • The creator prefers peeling and slicing the mango cleanly rather than scoring the cheek into cubes.
  • Coconut oil helps the white chocolate melt and coat more smoothly.
  • White chocolate sets very quickly on the frozen bites, so toppings need to be ready before dipping.

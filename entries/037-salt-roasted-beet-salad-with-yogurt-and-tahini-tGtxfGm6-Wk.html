Salt-Roasted Beet Salad with Yogurt and Tahini

Beets are roasted cut-side down on a thick bed of salt until tender, then peeled and paired with tahini, yogurt, lemon or lime, parsley, cumin, salt, and pepper. The salt roasting concentrates sweetness and reduces the earthy edge.

Instructions

  1. Heat the oven to 200°C.
  2. Wash the beets thoroughly and dry them as well as possible.
  3. Cut the beets in half.
  4. Pour a thick layer of salt onto a baking tray.
  5. Place the beets cut-side down on the salt. The salt layer should be thick enough to prevent beet juices from burning on the tray.
  6. Roast until the beets can be pierced with a fork, about 40-50 minutes depending on size.
  7. Rinse or wipe the beets to remove excess salt from the outside.
  8. Peel the skins with a knife.
  9. Make a salad with the roasted beets, tahini, yogurt, lemon or lime juice, parsley, cumin, salt, and pepper.

Notes

  • The creator says the salt can be reused.
  • The video suggests yogurt or soft cheese with a lot of acid as the preferred pairing.
  • The pinned comment also gives a simpler option: peel raw beets, cut into bite-size pieces, coat with olive oil and salt, and roast normally.

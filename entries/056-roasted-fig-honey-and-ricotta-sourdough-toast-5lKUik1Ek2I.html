Roasted Fig, Honey, and Ricotta Sourdough Toast

Fresh figs are roasted with honey and balsamic, then served warm over whipped ricotta on toasted sourdough with basil. The result is sweet, tangy, creamy, and simple but polished.

Instructions

  1. Heat the oven to a moderate roasting temperature.
  2. Cover the figs with honey and a little balsamic vinegar.
  3. Roast until softened and syrupy, about 25 minutes by the creator's estimate.
  4. Toast the sourdough.
  5. Whip the ricotta briefly to make it lighter.
  6. Spread whipped ricotta on the toast.
  7. Top with warm roasted figs.
  8. Finish with fresh basil.

Notes

  • The source describes the ricotta as the cold, milky base for the warm figs.
  • Basil is included because it pairs with the rich fig and ricotta combination.

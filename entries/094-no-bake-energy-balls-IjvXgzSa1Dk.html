No-Bake Energy Balls

A flexible no-bake oat and peanut butter snack formula with sticky sweetener, vanilla, coconut, and optional add-ins such as almonds and chia seeds.

Cookable Untested

Ingredients

  • 90 g quick oats
  • 130 g peanut butter
  • 110 g honey, maple syrup, or molasses
  • 5 ml vanilla extract, optional
  • 40 g shredded coconut
  • 30 g almonds, chopped, optional
  • 10 g chia seeds, optional
  • Extra peanut butter or sweetener, as needed if the mixture is too dry

Effort

1 hr 15 min including rest

2 / 5 difficulty

low cleanup · 2 / 5

Instructions

  1. Combine oats, peanut butter, and the sticky sweetener.
  2. Add vanilla if using.
  3. Stir in coconut and any optional add-ins.
  4. If the mixture is too dry to come together, add a little more peanut butter or sweetener.
  5. Refrigerate for about 1 hour so the oats absorb moisture.
  6. Roll into balls, or shape however you like.

Notes

  • Quick oats are easier to stir than thicker oats.
  • The video includes joke ingredients; use edible add-ins only.
  • The source frames the recipe as something sweet that tastes close to a candy bar while pretending to be healthy.

Sugar Walnut Pasta

A sweet childhood comfort dish: toasted walnuts are ground with sugar, then tossed with buttered pasta until the sugar just starts to melt.

Cookable Untested

Ingredients

For 2 servings:

  • 170 g dried tagliatelle, or fresh pasta
  • 28 g butter
  • About 100 g walnuts
  • About 50 g granulated sugar

Effort

20 min

1 / 5 difficulty

low cleanup · 2 / 5

Source

KWOOWK

2022-09-17

Instructions

  1. Toast the walnuts in a hot skillet for 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally, until fragrant. Do not burn them.
  2. Transfer the walnuts to a food processor and blend until completely ground.
  3. Mix the sugar into the ground walnuts.
  4. Cook the pasta in boiling water until al dente.
  5. Transfer the pasta directly to a pan with melted butter.
  6. Toss to coat with butter.
  7. Add a generous amount of the sugar-walnut mixture.
  8. Toss and cook for a few seconds, just until the sugar starts to melt.
  9. Plate and top with more sugar-walnut mixture.

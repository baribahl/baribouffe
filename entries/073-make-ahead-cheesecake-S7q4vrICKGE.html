Make-Ahead Cheesecake

A baked cheesecake with a graham cracker crust, cream cheese filling, lemon, vanilla, sour cream, and eggs added one by one, baked in a water bath and cooled slowly to reduce cracking.

Instructions

  1. Mix graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, sugar, and a small amount of salt.
  2. Press the crumb mixture into a greased 23 cm springform pan.
  3. Bake the crust until crisp, about 10 minutes.
  4. Beat cream cheese, 400 g sugar, and 25 g flour for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Scrape down the bowl.
  6. Add lemon zest, lemon juice, vanilla bean paste, more salt, and sour cream.
  7. Add the eggs one by one so the mixture does not split.
  8. Wrap the pan in foil.
  9. Set the pan in a bain-marie.
  10. Bake at 165 C for about 1 hr 30 min.
  11. Let the cheesecake cool in the oven with the door cracked.
  12. Cool completely before removing from the pan.
  13. Freeze if making it ahead.

Notes

  • Slow cooling in the oven is used to reduce cracking.
  • The creator says the cheesecake can be made a month or two in advance and stored frozen.

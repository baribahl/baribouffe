Before You Cook

Use caution

/!\ WARNING: Use clean equipment for batter fermentation and discard the batter if it develops off smells, mold, or other abnormal signs.

Needs details

/!\ DISCLAIMER: The video is a rating and overview, not a measured dosa recipe. The dosa batter ratio, water, spice mix, potato amount, and fermentation temperature need a tested source before review.

Instructions

  1. Rinse the rice and lentils thoroughly.
  2. Soak for 6 hours.
  3. Blend until a relatively smooth batter forms.
  4. Let ferment overnight, up to about 12 hours.
  5. Thin the batter with water as needed.
  6. Cook dosas on a hot pan, spreading the batter with the back of a ladle if possible.
  7. For the filling, start with a spice mixture and aromatics.
  8. Add cooked potatoes and a little water.
  9. Cook until the potatoes turn into a soft mash.
  10. Season with salt, lemon juice, and fresh coriander.
  11. Fill the dosas with the potato mixture.

Notes

  • Comments point out that sambar and coconut chutney are missing from the demonstrated breakfast plate.
  • The creator says he struggled to get the classic circular spreading pattern.

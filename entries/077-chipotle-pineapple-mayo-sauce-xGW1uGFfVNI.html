Chipotle Pineapple Mayo Sauce

A sweet, spicy, sour, and savory blended sauce built from chipotles in adobo, honey, lime, pineapple, cilantro, garlic, and mayonnaise.

Cookable Use caution

Ingredients

  • 1 can chipotle peppers in adobo, about 200 g
  • 21 g honey
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 57 g fresh pineapple
  • 28 g fresh cilantro
  • 5 g salt
  • Black pepper, a small pinch; source unit unclear
  • 3 g garlic powder
  • 115 g mayonnaise

Effort

10 min

Before You Cook

Use caution

/!\ WARNING: This is a mayonnaise-based sauce. Keep it refrigerated and do not leave it sitting warm for extended serving periods.

Instructions

  1. Add the chipotle peppers and adobo sauce to a blender.
  2. Add honey, lime juice, pineapple, cilantro, salt, black pepper, and garlic powder.
  3. Blend until smooth.
  4. Add the mayonnaise after blending the first ingredients.
  5. Blend or stir just until incorporated.
  6. Taste and adjust lime, salt, or sweetness.
  7. Refrigerate until serving.

Notes

  • The pineapple adds sweetness and acidity without making the sauce taste only sugary.
  • The creator adds mayonnaise after blending the base.
  • Use on tacos, grilled meat, vegetables, sandwiches, potatoes, or eggs.

Fire-Roasted Corn and Black Bean Scoopable Salad

A meal-prep "scoopable salad" built from fire-roasted corn, black beans, chopped vegetables, feta, light mayonnaise, lime, and pantry seasonings. It is meant to be eaten with tortilla chips or used as an easy way to add more vegetables.

Needs details

Ingredients

  • 2 bags frozen fire-roasted corn
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed [review: source typo says "I can"]
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 2 red bell peppers, diced
  • 1 shallot, diced
  • Feta cheese, to taste
  • Light mayonnaise, to taste
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Salt
  • Chili powder
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • 1.5 avocados, added just before eating if meal prepping

Optional:

  • Jalapeno, if you want heat
  • Plain Greek yogurt mixed with some mayonnaise as a partial mayo substitute
  • Tortilla chips, for serving

Before You Cook

Instructions

  1. Warm the frozen corn briefly if needed, then chill it.
  2. Dice the cucumber, red bell peppers, and shallot.
  3. Combine the corn, black beans, chopped vegetables, and feta.
  4. Add light mayonnaise.
  5. Add lime juice, salt, chili powder, garlic powder, and onion powder.
  6. Mix well.
  7. If meal prepping, add avocado only before serving so it does not brown.
  8. Serve with tortilla chips.

Notes

  • The source frames this as a high-volume, easy, repeatable meal-prep salad.
  • A veggie chopper makes the prep much faster but is not required.

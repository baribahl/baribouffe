Feta Tomato Pasta with Seared Chicken

A baked feta and grape tomato pasta sauce with oregano and chili flakes, served with pasta, seared chicken, and basil. The pinned recipe covers the tomato-feta bake; the video adds chicken and pasta assembly.

Needs details

Ingredients

For the tomato-feta base:

  • 450 g grape tomatoes
  • 115 g feta cheese
  • Olive oil, enough to coat
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste
  • About 1 g dried oregano
  • About 2 g chili flakes

For assembly:

  • Pasta, quantity not specified
  • Chicken breast, quantity not specified
  • Salt and pepper, for the chicken
  • Fresh basil, quantity not specified

Before You Cook

Needs details

/!\ DISCLAIMER: The source provides quantities for the baked tomato-feta base, but not for pasta, chicken, basil, or chicken seasoning. Keep those parts flexible until tested.

Instructions

  1. Heat the oven to 200°C.
  2. Put the grape tomatoes in a baking dish.
  3. Place the feta in the center.
  4. Drizzle with enough olive oil to coat.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, oregano, and chili flakes.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes, until the tomatoes blister and the feta softens.
  7. Meanwhile, pat the chicken dry.
  8. Cut the chicken breast in half for better surface area.
  9. Season with salt and pepper, then sear until cooked through.
  10. Cook the pasta.
  11. Smash the hot feta and tomatoes together, leaving some texture.
  12. Add the pasta, seared chicken, and basil.

Notes

  • The video frames the "Latin kick" mostly as the creator's own tasting commentary; the pinned recipe itself is oregano and chili flake.
  • A commenter says shrimp worked as a chicken substitute.

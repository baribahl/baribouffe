Two-Minute Homemade Mayonnaise

A quick blender mayonnaise made from one egg, lemon juice, vegetable oil, and optional salt. The oil is added gradually while blending until the emulsion turns thick and creamy.

Cookable Use caution

Ingredients

  • 1 pasteurized egg, preferred
  • 190 ml vegetable oil, for example 150 ml sunflower oil plus 40 ml olive oil
  • 15 ml lemon juice
  • Salt, optional
  • Dijon mustard, optional

Effort

2 min

Before You Cook

Use caution

/!\ WARNING: This mayonnaise uses raw egg. Use pasteurized egg if possible, refrigerate promptly, and use within 2-3 days as stated by the source.

Instructions

  1. Add the egg and lemon juice to a blender or narrow blending container.
  2. Blend at medium speed.
  3. Gradually drizzle in the oil while blending.
  4. Continue blending until thick and creamy.
  5. Add a pinch of salt if desired.
  6. Refrigerate promptly.

Notes

  • The creator prefers the recipe without Dijon mustard, but notes that some people add it.
  • A commenter notes that room-temperature ingredients, slow oil addition, and not overdoing lemon help the emulsion thicken.

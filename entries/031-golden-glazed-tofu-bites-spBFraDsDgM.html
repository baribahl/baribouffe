Golden Glazed Tofu Bites

Crispy torn tofu bites tossed in a sweet, lemony, spiced glaze. Tearing the tofu instead of cutting it gives irregular edges and more surface area for browning.

Cookable Untested

Ingredients

For the tofu:

  • 400 g extra-firm tofu
  • About 16 g cornstarch
  • About 5 g salt
  • Oil, for frying

For the glaze:

  • 60 ml agave syrup or maple syrup
  • Juice of 1 small lemon
  • About 2 g paprika
  • About 2 g ground coriander
  • About 2 g ground cumin
  • About 1 g chili powder
  • About 1 g garlic powder
  • Salt, to taste
  • 15-30 ml water

Effort

20-25 min

2 / 5 difficulty

medium cleanup · 3 / 5

Instructions

  1. Pat the tofu dry.
  2. Tear it into bite-sized pieces.
  3. Toss the tofu with cornstarch and salt.
  4. Fry in oil until crisp and golden.
  5. In a bowl, combine agave or maple syrup, lemon juice, paprika, ground coriander, cumin, chili powder, garlic powder, salt, and water.
  6. Pour the glaze over the crispy tofu.
  7. Toss until coated and serve immediately.

Notes

  • The uploader says extra-firm tofu is best.
  • Tearing the tofu is part of the texture strategy, not just a shortcut.
  • The glaze can use agave or maple syrup.

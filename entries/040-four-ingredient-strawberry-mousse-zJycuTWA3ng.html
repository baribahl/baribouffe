Four-Ingredient Strawberry Mousse

A simple strawberry mousse made by cooking blended strawberries with sugar and cornstarch into a thick base, chilling it, then folding it into whipped cream. It can be served as a mousse or used to decorate cakes.

Cookable Untested

Ingredients

  • 300 g strawberries
  • 50 g sugar
  • 35 g cornstarch
  • 140 ml whipping cream, dairy or plant-based
  • 5 ml vanilla extract, optional

Effort

about 90 min including chilling

2 / 5 difficulty

medium cleanup · 3 / 5

Source

Instructions

  1. Blend the strawberries with the sugar and cornstarch.
  2. Cook over medium heat for a few minutes, whisking constantly, until creamy and thickened.
  3. Remove from the heat.
  4. Mix in vanilla extract if using.
  5. Let cool slightly.
  6. Cover with plastic wrap in contact with the surface.
  7. Refrigerate for about 1 hour.
  8. Whisk the cream until peaks form.
  9. Gently fold the chilled strawberry mixture into the whipped cream.
  10. Serve with a piping bag or spoon.

Notes

  • The creator notes that the mousse can also be used to decorate cakes or other desserts.
  • A commenter suggests the same fruit base idea can work with peaches.

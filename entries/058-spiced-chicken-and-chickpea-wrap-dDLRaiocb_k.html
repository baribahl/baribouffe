Spiced Chicken and Chickpea Wrap

A high-protein wrap with spice-coated chicken tenders, crisp spiced chickpeas, a yogurt-tahini-garlic herb sauce, cucumber, red cabbage, and flatbread.

Instructions

  1. Season the chicken tenders heavily with za'atar or other spices.
  2. Add olive oil and lemon juice and rub the seasoning into the chicken.
  3. Cook the chicken in a pan until done.
  4. Chop the cooked chicken.
  5. Stir-fry chickpeas with spices for a few minutes until crisp.
  6. Make the sauce by whisking or blending yogurt, lemon or lime juice, garlic, tahini, herbs, salt, pepper, and spices.
  7. Warm the flatbread.
  8. Spread sauce on the bread.
  9. Add chicken, chickpeas, cucumber, red cabbage, and optional spicy cheese.
  10. Wrap and serve.

Notes

  • Comments emphasize that raw garlic in the sauce is much stronger than cooked garlic.
  • The source frames this as a more enjoyable alternative to bland "healthy" chicken and broccoli.

Healthy Chicken Quesadilla

A quick lunch quesadilla with seasoned chicken, salted tomato, Gouda, and a garlic-herb yogurt tahini sauce.

Needs details

Ingredients

For 1 serving:

  • 1/2 chicken breast
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste
  • Smoked paprika, to taste
  • Garlic powder, to taste

For the sauce:

  • 30 g Greek yogurt
  • About 10 g tahini
  • 1 small handful fresh coriander
  • 1 small handful fresh parsley
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste
  • Juice of 1/2 lime

For the quesadilla:

  • 2 medium tortillas
  • 1/2 large tomato, sliced and salted
  • Gouda cheese, sliced or grated, to taste

Before You Cook

Instructions

  1. Butterfly the chicken breast by cutting it through the middle and opening it like a book.
  2. Season both sides with salt, pepper, smoked paprika, and garlic powder.
  3. Cook the chicken in a medium-heat pan for 3-4 minutes per side, until done.
  4. Slice into bite-sized pieces.
  5. Blend Greek yogurt, tahini, coriander, parsley, garlic, salt, pepper, and lime juice into a sauce.
  6. Place salted tomato slices, chicken, sauce, and Gouda on one side of each tortilla.
  7. Fold each tortilla closed.
  8. Fry for about 2 minutes per side, until golden.

Notes

  • The video uses fresh tomato, chicken, a lot of sauce, and spicy cheese; the linked KWOOWK recipe specifies Gouda.
  • Some Samsung Food comments suggest reducing the raw garlic if sensitive.

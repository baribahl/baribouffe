Pakistani Nihari

A slow-braised lamb shank nihari: lamb is salted, browned, cooked with onion, ginger-garlic paste, nihari masala, and water until falling off the bone, then thickened and served with spicy oil, ginger, coriander, and green chili.

Instructions

  1. Salt the lamb shank.
  2. Brown it on all sides in a Dutch oven.
  3. Remove the meat.
  4. Add onion, ginger-garlic paste, and nihari masala.
  5. Toast the spices together.
  6. Return the meat to the pot.
  7. Cover with water.
  8. Braise on low heat in the oven for 4-5 hours, until tender and falling off the bone.
  9. Thicken the sauce with a wheat flour and water slurry.
  10. Serve with spicy oil, ginger, coriander, and green chili.

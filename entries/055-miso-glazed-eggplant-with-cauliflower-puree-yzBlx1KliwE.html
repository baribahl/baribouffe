Miso-Glazed Eggplant with Cauliflower Puree

A vegetarian date-night plate: scored eggplant is salted, seared, roasted, then brushed with a miso-soy-rice-vinegar glaze and served over cauliflower puree with toasted walnuts and scallions.

Cookable Untested

Ingredients

For the eggplant:

  • 1 eggplant, halved
  • Vegetable oil, as needed
  • Salt, to taste

For the glaze:

  • About 18 g miso paste
  • 15 ml soy sauce
  • 15 ml rice vinegar
  • About 12 g brown sugar
  • 10 ml toasted sesame oil

For the cauliflower puree:

  • 1/2 head cauliflower
  • Vegetable bouillon, optional
  • 100 ml heavy cream
  • 50 g cold butter
  • Ground nutmeg, to taste
  • Salt, to taste

For garnish:

  • Toasted walnuts
  • Scallions, sliced

75 min

Instructions

  1. Halve the eggplant and score the flesh in a crosshatch pattern without cutting through the skin.
  2. Salt the eggplant generously and let sit for 20 minutes to draw out moisture.
  3. Pat dry with paper towel.
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a medium-heat skillet.
  5. Cook the eggplant for 2-3 minutes per side, until slightly browned and softened.
  6. Transfer the eggplant to a rack and roast at 200°C for 15 minutes.
  7. Mix the miso paste, soy sauce, rice vinegar, brown sugar, and toasted sesame oil into a glaze.
  8. Brush the eggplant generously with the glaze.
  9. Return to the oven for 10-15 minutes, until browned on top but not burnt.
  10. For the puree, chop the cauliflower into large pieces.
  11. Boil in water, optionally with vegetable bouillon, for 15-20 minutes until very soft.
  12. Blend the cauliflower.
  13. Gradually add heavy cream and cold butter until smooth.
  14. Season with salt and nutmeg.
  15. Plate the glazed eggplant over cauliflower puree.
  16. Finish with toasted walnuts and sliced scallions.

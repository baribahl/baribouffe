Macadamia Snack Balls

No-bake snack balls made from macadamia nut butter, maple syrup, coconut flour, rolled oats, freeze-dried cranberries, macadamia nuts, and enough water to bind.

Needs details

Ingredients

  • 250 g macadamia nut butter
  • 60 g maple syrup
  • 55 g coconut flour
  • 40 g rolled oats
  • About 10-15 g freeze-dried cranberries
  • About 45 g macadamia nuts
  • Water, as needed for consistency

Effort

20 min plus optional chilling

2 / 5 difficulty

low cleanup · 2 / 5

Before You Cook

Needs details

/!\ DISCLAIMER: The description gives the full recipe, but appears to omit the unit for freeze-dried cranberries and macadamia nuts. Those are interpreted as small volume measures and converted approximately; verify before marking reviewed.

Instructions

  1. Combine macadamia nut butter and maple syrup.
  2. Mix in coconut flour and rolled oats.
  3. Add freeze-dried cranberries and macadamia nuts.
  4. Add water a little at a time until the mixture holds together.
  5. Roll into snack balls.
  6. Chill if the mixture is soft.

Notes

  • The video frames the snack as a more filling alternative to chips or cookies.
  • Coconut flour absorbs moisture strongly, so add water slowly.
  • Chilling should make the balls firmer and less sticky.

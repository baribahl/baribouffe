Microwave Mayo Omelette

A very fast microwave omelette inspired by Jose Andres: whisk one egg with full-fat mayonnaise, add salted butter, microwave briefly, and garnish with parmesan.

Cookable Untested

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 15 g full-fat mayonnaise
  • 5 g salted butter
  • Parmesan, for garnish

Effort

3 min

1 / 5 difficulty

low cleanup · 1 / 5

Source

The Plant Slant

2025-08-29

Instructions

  1. Whisk the egg with the mayonnaise until combined.
  2. Add the salted butter to a microwave-safe bowl or small dish.
  3. Add the egg mixture.
  4. Microwave for 30-40 seconds, stopping when just set.
  5. Garnish with parmesan.

Notes

  • The mayonnaise adds fat and helps create a soft texture.
  • Use a short cook time first; overcooked microwave eggs turn rubbery quickly.

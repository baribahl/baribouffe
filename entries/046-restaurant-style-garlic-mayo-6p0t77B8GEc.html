Restaurant-Style Garlic Mayo

A garlic mayonnaise made by emulsifying garlic, mustard, lemon juice, egg yolk, salt, and neutral oil. The mustard supports emulsification, while neutral oil keeps the flavor cleaner than olive oil.

Cookable Use caution

Ingredients

  • 2 garlic cloves, grated
  • About 5 g mustard
  • 10 ml fresh lemon juice
  • 1 egg yolk, pasteurized if possible
  • About 5 g salt, or lemon pepper seasoning as an optional variation
  • 240 ml neutral oil, such as avocado, soybean, or peanut oil

Effort

10 min

Before You Cook

Use caution

/!\ WARNING: This sauce uses raw egg yolk. Use pasteurized egg if possible, refrigerate promptly, and do not leave it at room temperature.

Instructions

  1. Grate the garlic into a mixing bowl.
  2. Add mustard, lemon juice, egg yolk, and salt.
  3. Mix well before adding oil.
  4. Slowly drizzle in the neutral oil while whisking or blending constantly.
  5. Continue until the mayonnaise is thick and emulsified.

Notes

  • The uploader clarifies that neutral oil means a low-flavor oil such as avocado, soybean, or peanut oil.
  • The source distinguishes garlic mayonnaise from true aioli because this version uses egg yolk.

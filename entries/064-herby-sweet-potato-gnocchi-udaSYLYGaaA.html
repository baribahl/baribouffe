Herby Sweet Potato Gnocchi

Sweet potatoes and flour form a simple vegan gnocchi dough, served with a parsley, cashew, garlic, lemon, and olive oil sauce loosened with gnocchi water.

Needs details

Ingredients

For 3 servings:

  • 450 g sweet potatoes
  • 120-180 g flour, plus more for dusting
  • Salt, to taste

For the sauce:

  • Large handful parsley
  • Cashews, quantity not specified
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 60 ml olive oil
  • Gnocchi cooking water, as needed

Optional:

  • Lemon zest
  • Feta, if not keeping the dish vegan

Before You Cook

Needs details

/!\ DISCLAIMER: The gnocchi quantities are available from the linked KWOOWK recipe, but the cashew amount in the sauce is not specified. Treat the sauce as flexible until tested.

Instructions

  1. Pierce the sweet potatoes with a fork.
  2. Bake at 190°C for about 60 minutes, or until soft inside.
  3. Halve the sweet potatoes and scoop the flesh into a bowl.
  4. Add salt.
  5. Gradually mix in flour, starting with about 120 g and adding more as needed.
  6. Knead on a well-floured surface until the dough is soft and no longer sticky.
  7. Divide the dough into 4 pieces.
  8. Roll each piece into a thin rope.
  9. Cut into small gnocchi pieces and dust lightly with flour.
  10. Blend parsley, cashews, garlic, and olive oil into a thick sauce.
  11. Cook the gnocchi in salted boiling water until they float.
  12. In a hot pan, mix sauce with a ladle of gnocchi cooking water.
  13. Toss the cooked gnocchi in the sauce until coated.
  14. Serve with lemon zest and optional feta.

Notes

  • The linked recipe emphasizes dough texture over exact flour quantity.
  • Samsung Food comments note the dough can be sticky because sweet potatoes are moist.
